\cvitem{Abstract}{
We studied 43 White-light flares (WLF) using the satellite data from the Solar Dynamic Observatory concluding that the applied methods on the study of these processes generate artefacts which produce flux overestimation during WLFs. At this point, we proposed a new method which achieves considerable progress respect to classical methods. In addition, we analysed the WLFs writing a radiative transfer code to simulate the $H^-$ ion emission and the opacities of H, Si, C concluding that the Backwarming model is able to heat the lower solar atmosphere to produce the white-light emission.}
